SharedCopy + WebMarker

After showing SharedCopy to some people at the Geekout, Vu Xuan Linh pointed me to the Web Marker firefox extension. My first thought was, argh… feature rat race…

But after installing and seeing how the extension works, I’m happy to say that the two can co-exist quite happily. What other tools are out there that could be complimentary to the service?

Progress update:
