Yanime In SourceForge

At last - some version control! Yanime is now hosted in SourceForge. Feel free to checkout from the CVS (Anonymous).

cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/yanime login


When prompted for a password, simply press the Enter key


cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/yanime co -P yanime


You can use any CVS client, I like TortoiseCVS for its convenient UI.
