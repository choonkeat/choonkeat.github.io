I'm glad to announce that RssFwd's hosting will henceforth be sponsored by Sproutit.com [see story]

To pacify the most important question: Yes, RssFwd will continue to be free. Hey, its an open source software ya know?

What's so hi-tech and advanced about RssFwd? Nothing! Charles Jolley and gang appreciates RssFwd precisely for its nothingness - what it doesn't do and the little it already does - no lock-in, no lock-out, no user accounts and frivolous login/passwords to remember, it is simple and out of your way [pdf, recommended reading]

Go read the official announcement! It can only get better!
